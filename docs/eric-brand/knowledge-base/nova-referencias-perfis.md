## 2. ALFREDO SOARES (@alfredosoares) -- Entrepreneur, VTEX Co-founder, G4 Educacao

### Biography

Alfredo Soares was born June 8, 1987, in Rio de Janeiro. He grew up in the Tijuca neighborhood and was a federado athlete in water polo, receiving an athlete scholarship from Fluminense football club to study. His father was a general manager at a cash transport company, instilling discipline and focus from a young age. He graduated in Social Communication with emphasis in Advertising.

**Career timeline:**

- **Age 17**: First business -- "Ideia Carioca" with friend Reinaldo, creating and selling business cards to taxi drivers and small merchants. Lasted almost 2 years.
- **Age 19**: Started selling business cards door-to-door in Rio's North Zone
- **~2014**: Founded **Xtech Commerce**, an e-commerce platform. In 3 years, the platform transacted over **R$ 547 million** in sales.
- **2017**: Xtech was **acquired by VTEX** (Latin America's largest e-commerce platform) for **R$ 14 million**. Alfredo became partner and VP of Institutional Relations.
- **2019**: Co-founded **G4 Educacao** with Tallis Gomes (founder of Easy Taxi) and Bruno Nardon (founder of Rappi Brasil). The G4 became Brazil's leading business education platform for SMB owners.
- **Current**: President of **Loja Integrada**, co-founder/mentor at G4 Educacao, author of 4 best-sellers, 1 million Instagram followers, 300+ lectures at major events across Brazil.

1. **Vender e um processo** (Selling is a process): Don't just offer the product -- understand audience demands and generate buying interest

3. **Ser mais interessante do que interesseiro** (Be more interesting than interested): Create relevant content that adds value before asking for the sale
4. **Atitude acima de inteligencia** (Attitude above intelligence): Hard work, persistence, and going to the street matters more than being smart
5. **Nao espere o momento perfeito** (Don't wait for the perfect moment): The ideal day to start selling is today
6. **A grande empresa e a pequena empresa que fez a coisa certa** (The big company is the small company that did the right thing)

- **Ecossistema de Vendas (Sales Ecosystem)**: Built on 3 pillars -- **Canal** (Channel), **Produto** (Product), **Persona**

3. **Behavioral mastery before tactical mastery**: You must develop the internal profile before learning techniques

  6. (6th pillar related to metrics/management)
- **Social Selling Funnels**: Using social media as a conversion channel, not just branding

- Reels showing closing techniques and mindset shifts

- Co-hosts the Full Sales System Cast podcast
